Our Story

Mau Tours and Taxis was born from a desire to share the real Zanzibar – beyond the postcards. We started with two cars and a dream. Today, we are proud to have served thousands of happy travellers, from solo adventurers to large families.

We believe tourism should benefit local communities and preserve nature. That's why we support small spice farms, eco‑friendly hotels, and give back to local schools. When you travel with us, you're not just a tourist – you're part of the Mau family.
